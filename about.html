ABOUT

Hello, I'm Malcolm Lismore, a freelance photographer based on the stunning North West coast of Scotland.
My passion for photography is deeply rooted in my love for the natural world.
Growing up surrounded by the rugged Scottish landscape, I developed a fascination for capturing its raw beauty through my lens.
Over the years, I've dedicated myself to photographing the diverse wildlife and breathtaking coastal scenes that make this region so special.
My work is driven by a desire to share the wonders of nature with others, and I strive to convey the majesty and tranquility
of the natural world in every photograph. In addition to my nature photography, I also enjoy capturing life's special moments,
from weddings and portraits to various events. Photography, for me, is not just about taking pictures but about telling stories and preserving memories.
